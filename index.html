I am a master student working on Human-Computer Interaction at the EMC (Université Lumière Lyon 2).

My research goal is to investigate how AI-driven technologies, ranging from smart mobility solutions to complex automated industrial interfaces, affect human cognitive states, such as the sense of agency and trust. I aim to contribute to the development of next-generation Human-Centered tool, ensuring that highly advanced machines remain intuitive and safe for the user.

I am currently seeking a CIFRE PhD position (2026–2029) at the intersection of Cognitive Sciences and the Industry.
